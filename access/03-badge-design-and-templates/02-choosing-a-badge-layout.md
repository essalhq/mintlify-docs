Essal Access includes 7 built-in badge layouts. Each has a distinct visual style and arrangement of elements. This article describes each layout and when to use it.

Navigate to **Badge Designer** in the sidebar. The **Templates** section is at the top of the left panel.

![Badge Designer showing the layout selector with 7 template options](../screenshots/badge-designer.png)

---

## Selecting a Layout

Click any layout button in the **Templates** section. The badge preview in the center panel updates immediately. The selected layout is highlighted with a blue border and a checkmark.

Your choice is saved when you click **Save** in the right panel header.

---

## The 7 Built-In Layouts

### Modern

A clean, contemporary layout with a distinctive left sidebar in your primary color, a circular employee photo, and QR code beside it. The right area is white with the company logo at the top, followed by the employee's name, a role pill, department, and other enabled fields.

**Best for**: Professional services, technology companies, modern workplaces that want a sleek, minimal look.

---

### Corporate

A structured, formal layout with a full-width primary-color header bar containing the company logo and employee ID. The body shows the employee photo beside their name, role, and department. A QR code sits at the bottom right with a colored accent stripe.

Available in both landscape and portrait arrangements:

- **Landscape** — horizontal photo and details side by side
- **Portrait** — circular photo centered below the header, details in a floating card

**Best for**: Large enterprises, banks, hospitals, government institutions — anywhere a formal, hierarchical design is expected.

---

### Tech

A dark-themed layout with a near-black gradient background. The employee photo is styled in grayscale with high contrast and a green "active" status dot. Text is white and light-gray on dark. The employee's last name appears in your primary color. Field labels are minimal and uppercase.

**Best for**: IT departments, security operations, tech startups, or organizations where a modern, digital aesthetic is preferred.

---

### Conference

A versatile layout designed for events and large gatherings. The employee's name is displayed in a very large, bold font — optimized for legibility at a glance across a room. A vertical strip in your primary color runs down the left edge.

Available in landscape and portrait:

- **Landscape** — photo floats to the right, large name on the left
- **Portrait** — photo is centered, very large name below it with a bold role pill

**Best for**: Conferences, trade shows, events, trade fairs — anywhere fast visual identification from a distance is important.

---

### Minimal

A clean white layout with no heavy decorative elements. The photo and logo are small and unobtrusive. Content is arranged in a simple vertical flow with a thin primary-color accent bar. Maximum information density in a restrained design.

**Best for**: Organizations that prefer understated design, or where badge printing costs require minimizing ink coverage.

---

### Gradient

A bold, colorful layout with a full-badge gradient flowing from your primary color to your accent color. Decorative pulse orbs add depth. The QR code sits in a glassmorphic container. The employee's name and role are displayed in large white text. Badge ID and Employee ID appear in glassy chips at the bottom.

**Best for**: Creative agencies, media companies, events, hospitality — anywhere visual impact matters more than formality.

---

### Executive

A sophisticated dark-slate layout with subtle gold (amber) accents. A horizontal amber line runs across the top. The employee photo has a gold glow border. The company logo is rendered in amber. An `Executive` label with an animated amber dot marks the badge type.

**Best for**: C-suite and senior leadership badges, VIP passes, financial institutions, or any scenario requiring a premium, distinctive badge.

## Layout Comparison

| Layout     | Background           | Photo style              | Tone              |
| ---------- | -------------------- | ------------------------ | ----------------- |
| Modern     | White                | Circle, left sidebar     | Contemporary      |
| Corporate  | White + color header | Square/circle, prominent | Formal            |
| Tech       | Dark gradient        | Grayscale + contrast     | Modern, digital   |
| Conference | White                | Circle, floating         | Event-focused     |
| Minimal    | White                | Small circle             | Clean, restrained |
| Gradient   | Full color gradient  | Circle with glow         | Bold, creative    |
| Executive  | Dark slate           | Circle with gold glow    | Premium, luxury   |

The **My Business Card** page lets you manage the links that appear on your digital business card and preview exactly how your card looks to others.

![Business card editor](../screenshots/portal-business-card.png)

## What Is a Digital Business Card

Your digital business card is a shareable page that displays your professional details — name, role, department, contact information, bio, and any custom links you add. It can be shared via a link or accessed through your badge's QR code.

## Editing Custom Links

The left panel of the business card page contains the links editor.

### Adding a Link

1. Select a **Type** from the dropdown:
   - Website
   - LinkedIn
   - Twitter / X
   - Instagram
   - Facebook
   - Custom
2. Enter the full URL in the **URL** field.
3. If you chose **Custom**, enter a **Label** that describes the link.
4. Click **Add** to add the link to your card.

### Removing a Link

Click the remove (×) button next to any link to delete it from your card.

### Saving

Click **Save** to apply your changes. Updates are reflected immediately in the preview and on your live business card.

## Live Preview

The right panel shows a real-time preview of your business card. It updates as you make changes.

Your card displays:

- Cover section with your company's brand colour
- Company logo and your profile photo
- Your full name, job title, and department
- Social media icons for any social links you have added
- **Save Contact** button — downloads a vCard so people can save your details to their phone
- **Email** and **Call** buttons (if your email and phone are on file)
- Any custom links you have added
- Your bio

## Sharing Your Business Card

Your business card is accessible at the URL linked to your badge. You can share this link directly, or let someone scan your QR code from the Home page, which takes them to the same page.

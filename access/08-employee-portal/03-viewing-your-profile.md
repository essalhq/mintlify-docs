The **My Profile** page shows all of your employment details. Some of this information is read-only and can only be changed by an administrator; other fields can be edited directly by you.

![Employee profile page](../screenshots/portal-profile.png)

## Read-Only Employment Details

At the top of the profile page you will see a summary of your employment record. These fields are maintained by your administrator and cannot be changed from the portal:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Employee ID** | Your unique identifier in the system |
| **Badge ID** | The ID of your currently active badge |
| **Role / Job Title** | Your position as recorded by HR |
| **Department** | Your assigned department |
| **Status** | Your current employment status |
| **Company** | The organisation you belong to |

## Your Profile Photo

Your current profile photo appears at the top of the page. If no photo has been uploaded, a placeholder is shown.

## Editable Personal Information

Below the read-only section you will find fields you can update yourself:

- First name and last name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Address and city
- Nationality
- Bio / summary

For full instructions on updating these fields, see Editing Your Profile.

## Custom Attributes

If your organisation has set up custom profile fields, they will appear in a separate section on the profile page. Fields that are marked as employee-editable can be filled in; others are shown as read-only if a value has already been entered by an administrator.

## Health and Safety

A dedicated **Health & Safety** section on the profile page stores emergency contact details, allergies, medical conditions, and disability information. See Updating Health and Safety Information for details.

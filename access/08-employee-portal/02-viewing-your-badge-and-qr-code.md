{/* category: Employee Portal */}

The **Home** page is the first thing you see after logging in. It displays an interactive 3D preview of your current badge and gives you quick access to your QR code.

![Employee Portal home page](../screenshots/portal-home.png)

## Your 3D Badge Preview

Your badge is shown as an interactive 3D card. You can tilt and rotate it by moving your mouse over it (or by tilting your device if you are on a mobile). Tap or click the badge to flip it and see the back.

The badge reflects your current design and personal information as configured by your administrator.

## Showing Your QR Code

1. Click **Show QR Code** on the home page.
2. A full-screen QR code appears.
3. Let someone scan it, or scan it with a check-in device, to verify your badge.
4. Click **Done** to close.

The QR code links to your badge verification page and is always up to date.

## Quick Actions

The home page also provides shortcuts:

- **My Profile** — jump to your profile editing page
- **Show QR Code** — open the full-screen QR code
- **Report Issue** — go to the Report Missing Badge page

## If Your Badge Is Not Showing

If the home page shows an empty state or no badge preview, your badge may not have been assigned yet or it may have been deactivated. Contact your HR or IT administrator to have a badge created and assigned to your account.

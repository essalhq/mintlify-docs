The Business Card Editor lets administrators set the visual style and content of business cards for all employees in the organization. Changes apply globally — every employee's card is updated.

![Business Card Editor](../screenshots/business-card-editor.png)

## Opening the Editor

Go to **Settings → Business Card Editor** in the admin navigation, or navigate to `/business-card-editor`. A live preview on the right shows how the card will look using real or sample employee data.

## Branding

**Primary color** — sets the color of the cover strip at the top of the card and the **Save Contact** button. Click the color swatch to open a color picker.

**Cover photo** — optionally upload a background image for the cover strip. Recommended dimensions: 1200 × 400 px or wider. The photo is compressed automatically on upload (max 1200 px wide). Click **Remove** to revert to the solid color background.

## Visibility Toggles

Control which sections appear on every employee's card:

| Toggle | What it controls |
|---|---|
| **Cover** | Colored/photo header strip |
| **Profile photo** | Employee's uploaded photo |
| **Role** | Job title |
| **Department** | Department name |
| **Contact info** | Master toggle for email and phone links |
| **Email** | Show email address and mailto link (requires contact toggle) |
| **Phone** | Show phone number and tel link (requires contact toggle) |
| **Location** | City and address |
| **Bio** | Employee bio / summary |

## Organization-Wide Custom Links

You can add links that appear on **every** employee's business card — for example, a company website, LinkedIn company page, or booking link.

### Adding a Link

1. In the **Custom Links** section, select a **Type**:
   - **Website** — shows as a pill button with a globe icon
   - **LinkedIn** — shows as a social icon circle (LinkedIn brand color)
   - **Twitter / X** — shows as a social icon circle
   - **Instagram** — shows as a social icon circle
   - **Facebook** — shows as a social icon circle
   - **Custom** — shows as a pill button; you can set a custom label
2. Enter the URL.
3. For **Custom** type, enter a label (e.g. "Book a Meeting").
4. Click **Add**.

### Removing a Link

Click the trash icon next to any link to remove it from all cards immediately.

## Saving Changes

Click **Save** to apply all changes. The live preview on the right reflects your edits in real time before saving.

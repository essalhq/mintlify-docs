Every employee in Essal Access has a digital business card — a clean, shareable page showing their professional details and contact information. The business card is designed to be shared like a physical card: via a link, a QR code, or a vCard file that can be saved directly to a phone's contacts.

![Business Card Editor](../screenshots/business-card-editor.png)

## Business Card vs. Public Profile

Essal Access has two different public-facing views:

| | Business Card | Full Public Profile |
|---|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Professional networking, contact sharing | Badge verification and access control |
| **Visible when** | Always (when business card mode is active) | Only when public verification is enabled |
| **Contains** | Name, role, department, contact, bio, links | All of the above + tickets, access zones, health & safety data |
| **Fallback** | Shown when full verification is disabled | — |

The business card is the lightweight, privacy-friendly view. The full public profile is the access-control-oriented view.

## What Appears on the Card

A business card shows:

- **Cover strip** — your organization's primary color, with optional cover photo and company logo
- **Profile photo** — employee's photo, or initials if no photo is set
- **Name, role, and department**
- **Social media icons** — LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook (linked)
- **Save Contact button** — downloads a vCard (.vcf) file for saving to phone contacts
- **Email and Call buttons** — quick-action links (if contact details are shared)
- **Website and custom links** — any additional links configured by the admin or employee
- **Bio** — employee's bio text
- **Contact section** — email and phone with icons

## Who Can Edit the Card

- **Administrators** configure the template (colors, cover photo, which fields are shown, and organization-wide links) in the **Business Card Editor**.
- **Employees** can add their own custom links from the Employee Portal.

## Enabling Business Card Mode

To route all badge scans to the business card instead of the full profile:

1. Go to **Settings → General**.
2. Disable **Allow public badge verification**.
3. Enable **Business card mode**.

With this configuration, scanning any employee badge shows the business card rather than a blocked/restricted screen.

Welcome to the **Essal Support Center**. Essal provides a unified suite of business software solutions designed to run your entire organization.

Whether you are configuring smart employee badges in **Essal Access**, managing document workflows in **Essal Office**, or monitoring system logs in **Essal Guard**, this support center is designed to help you get the most out of your tools.

## The Essal Suite

We support six primary applications:

1. **Essal Office**: Real-time collaborative documents, sheets, and presentations.
2. **Essal Access**: Identity provider, SSO, physical badges, and check-in device pairing.
3. **Essal Sales**: Customer relationship management, lead capture, and sales pipelines.
4. **Essal Careers**: Applicant tracking system and job board management.
5. **Essal Project**: Agile task boards, sprint planning, and employee time tracking.
6. **Essal Guard**: Central security dashboard, access policies, and audit logs.

## Finding Answers

- **Browse by Product**: Click on the tabs at the top of the page to access dedicated guides and troubleshooting resources for each specific product.
- **Search**: Use the search bar (press `Cmd+K` or `Ctrl+K`) to search across all support articles simultaneously.
- **Direct Support**: If you cannot find what you are looking for, contact our support team.

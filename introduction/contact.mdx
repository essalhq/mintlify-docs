If you have questions, run into issues, or need help with any of our products, our support team is here to assist.

## Contact Methods

- **Email Support**: You can email us directly at [support@essal.cloud](mailto:support@essal.cloud).
- **Admin Dashboard**: If you are logged into your organization's admin panel, you can click the **Help** or **Support** icon in the bottom corner to send us a direct message.

## Service Hours & Response Times

We provide support during standard business hours:
- **Hours**: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (CET)
- **Response Time**: We aim to respond to all standard support tickets within 24 hours. Critical security or system outages are monitored 24/7 and addressed immediately.

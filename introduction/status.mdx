You can check the real-time operational status of all Essal Cloud services at any time.

## Status Page

Visit the official **[Essal Status Page](https://status.essal.cloud)** to check for:
- Live uptime statistics for each SaaS application.
- Active system maintenance windows.
- Incident logs and historical outage details.

## Subscribing to Updates

From our status page, you can click **Subscribe to Updates** to receive email or SMS notifications automatically whenever there is an active incident or a scheduled maintenance window.
